---
title: Sign Up & First Steps
description: Create your Huginn account and have your first conversation in minutes.
---

Huginn uses Google to sign you in — there's no separate password to create or manage. Once you're in, your account is created automatically and you're ready to start.

## Create your account

<Steps>
<Step>
### Go to the Huginn dashboard

Navigate to the Huginn web app and you'll land on the sign-in page.

</Step>
<Step>
### Sign in with Google

Click **Continue with Google** and complete the Google OAuth flow. Huginn requests only your basic profile — your name and email address.

<Callout type="info">
  Huginn creates your account automatically on your first sign-in. No extra registration step is
  needed.
</Callout>

</Step>
<Step>
### Explore your dashboard

After signing in you'll land on the **Dashboard**, which gives you an overview of your account, quick links to key features, and the current state of your personality and connected channels.

</Step>
</Steps>

## Have your first conversation

Once you're signed in, head straight to **Chat** to say hello. Huginn is ready to talk immediately — even before you customize anything.

<Steps>
<Step>
### Open Chat

Click **Chat** in the navigation bar, or use the **Open Chat** quick action from the dashboard.

</Step>
<Step>
### Send a message

Type anything and press Enter (or click the send button). Huginn replies in real time with a streaming response — you'll see words appear as they're generated.

</Step>
<Step>
### Keep going

Every message in a session is part of the same conversation thread. Huginn uses the full context of the conversation to give coherent, relevant replies.

</Step>
</Steps>
