---
title: Dashboard Overview
description: Understand what the Huginn dashboard shows and how to navigate the app.
---

The dashboard is your central hub. It gives you a snapshot of your account, your personality files, your connected channels, and quick access to every feature.

## Navigation

The top navigation bar is present on every page. It gives you access to:

| Link            | What it opens                        |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **Chat**        | The real-time conversation interface |
| **Personality** | The SOUL and IDENTITY file editor    |
| **Channels**    | Your connected messaging channels    |
| **Calendars**   | Your linked calendar accounts        |
| **Dashboard**   | This overview page                   |

## Account Details panel

The left sidebar on the dashboard shows your account information:

- **Email** — your Google account email
- **Display Name** — your name from Google
- **Instance ID** — your unique Huginn account ID (useful for support)
- **Status** — always Online when you're signed in

## Quick Actions panel

Below the account details you'll find one-click shortcuts to the most common tasks: opening the chat, managing channels, editing your personality, and connecting a calendar.

## Personality summary

The main area shows a live preview of your current SOUL and IDENTITY files. If you haven't set them up yet, you'll see a prompt to get started. Once you've saved content, the snippet displays the first few lines so you can quickly confirm your personality is loaded.

<Callout type="info">
  The dashboard pulls the **latest version** of each file. Personality files are versioned — every
  save creates a new version, and the dashboard always shows the most recent one.
</Callout>

## Connected Channels panel

The right side of the dashboard shows which channels are connected. A green badge means a channel is linked; a grey badge means it isn't. Click **Connect** next to any channel to begin the linking flow.

## Sign out

Click **Sign Out** in the navigation or from the dashboard to end your session. Your data is preserved — signing back in restores everything.
