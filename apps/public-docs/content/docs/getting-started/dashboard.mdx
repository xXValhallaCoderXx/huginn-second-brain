title: Dashboard Overview
description: Understand what the Huginn dashboard shows and how to navigate the app.
---

The dashboard is your home base. When you sign in, it greets you by name, shows your calendar events for today, and gives you quick shortcuts to common tasks.

## Navigation

The top navigation bar is always visible. It links to three areas:

| Link           | What it opens                                                |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Dashboard**  | This overview page                                           |
| **Chat**       | The real-time conversation interface                         |
| **Settings**   | Personality, Channels, Calendars, and Account management     |

<Callout type="info">
  On mobile, navigation appears as a bottom tab bar with the same three destinations.
</Callout>

## Dashboard layout

The dashboard uses a two-column layout on larger screens and stacks to a single column on mobile.

### Left column

**Greeting card** — Shows a time-aware greeting ("Good morning", "Good afternoon", or "Good evening") with your display name and a live **Huginn is online** indicator.

**Quick Actions** — One-click shortcuts to the three most common tasks:

- **Chat with Huginn** — opens the conversation interface
- **Edit Personality** — jumps directly to the Personality tab in Settings
- **Manage Channels** — jumps directly to the Channels tab in Settings

**Personality Snapshot** — Shows the opening portion of your active SOUL file so you can quickly confirm your personality is loaded. Only appears once you have a SOUL file saved. Click **View full personality file** to go to the Personality editor.

**Account Details** — A collapsible section showing your Account ID and email address. For full account management, go to **Settings > Account**.

### Right column

**Today's Agenda** — Lists all your calendar events for today in a timeline view. Each event shows its title, time range (or "All day"), description, and location when available.

If you have no calendar connected, an empty state appears with a **Connect a calendar** link that takes you straight to the Calendars tab in Settings.

<Callout type="info">
  Calendar data is cached and refreshes approximately every 5 minutes. If a recently-added event
  isn't showing yet, reload the page after a moment.
</Callout>

## Settings

All configuration is collected under a single **Settings** page in the top navigation. It has four tabs:

| Tab             | What it manages                                                  |
| --------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Personality** | Edit your SOUL and IDENTITY files                                |
| **Channels**    | Connect and manage messaging channels (Telegram)                 |
| **Calendars**   | Connect and manage Google Calendar accounts                      |
| **Account**     | Profile information, Instance ID, and Sign Out                   |

You can navigate directly to a specific tab — for example, the Quick Actions on the dashboard link directly to the Personality and Channels tabs.

## Account tab

The **Account** tab inside Settings shows:

- **Profile card** — your avatar initial, display name, email address, and a **Google OAuth** label confirming your sign-in method.
- **Instance ID** — your unique Huginn identifier. Use the copy button to copy it quickly if you ever need it for support.
- **Sign Out** — ends your session on this device. Your data and personality files are preserved — signing back in restores everything.
