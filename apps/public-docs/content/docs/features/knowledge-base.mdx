Huginn keeps a personal knowledge base for you. As you chat, it quietly captures things worth remembering — a preference you mentioned, a decision you made, a fact you shared. You can also tell Huginn to save something explicitly, search and browse everything it knows about you, and create notes directly from the dashboard.

The same knowledge base is accessible everywhere: notes saved via Telegram show up on the web, and notes you create on the dashboard are available when chatting on Telegram.

## Automatic knowledge capture

During normal conversation, Huginn notices when you share something meaningful — a preference, a goal, a decision, a recurring fact about your life — and saves it as a note without interrupting the flow of the conversation. When it does, it briefly acknowledges: **"I noted that down."**

You don't need to do anything for this to happen. Just chat naturally and Huginn builds up your knowledge base over time.

<Callout type="info">
  Huginn is selective about what it captures. It saves things that are likely to be useful later,
  not a transcript of everything you said.
</Callout>

## Asking Huginn to remember something

If you want to make sure something is saved, just tell Huginn directly:

- "Remember that I prefer morning meetings."
- "Save this: my emergency contact is Jordan, +1-555-0123."
- "Note that we decided to go with the blue colour scheme."

Huginn will confirm with a short acknowledgement and save the note immediately.

## Recalling saved knowledge

You can ask Huginn to search your knowledge base in natural language at any time:

- "What do you know about my diet preferences?"
- "Do you have anything saved about my home network setup?"
- "What did I tell you about the project deadline?"

Huginn searches your saved notes and surfaces the most relevant ones in its answer.

## The Knowledge Base page

The **Knowledge Base** page in the dashboard gives you a full view of everything that's been saved. You can reach it from the **Knowledge** link in the top navigation bar, between Chat and Settings.

### Searching and filtering

At the top of the page you'll find a search bar and filter controls. You can:

- **Search by keyword** — type any word or phrase to filter notes by title and content.
- **Filter by tag** — click any tag pill to show only notes with that tag. Click again to clear the filter.
- **Filter by source** — show only notes captured by Huginn (sparkle icon) or notes you created manually (bookmark icon).

Filters stack: you can combine a keyword search with a tag filter at the same time.

### Creating a note manually

<Steps>
<Step>
### Open the Knowledge Base page

Click **Knowledge** in the top navigation bar to go to the Knowledge Base page.

</Step>
<Step>
### Click New Note

Click the **New Note** button in the top-right corner of the page. A blank note form will appear.

</Step>
<Step>
### Fill in the details

Enter a **title** and the note **content**. You can optionally add **tags** to help organise and filter your notes later.

</Step>
<Step>
### Save the note

Click **Save** to create the note. It will appear in your note list immediately.

</Step>
</Steps>

### Editing a note

Click on any note to open it in inline edit mode. You can update the title and content directly. Changes are saved when you click **Save**.

### Deleting a note

Each note has a **Delete** button. Clicking it will show an inline confirmation step so you don't accidentally remove something. Confirm to permanently delete the note.

<Callout type="warn">
  Deleted notes cannot be recovered. If you're unsure, consider editing the note to mark it as
  outdated rather than deleting it.
</Callout>

## Dashboard preview

The main dashboard shows a **Saved Knowledge** card with your five most recently added notes. This gives you a quick glance at what's been captured recently without navigating to the full Knowledge Base page.

## Understanding note metadata

Every note shows:

| Field              | What it means                                                                                        |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Title**          | A short label for the note                                                                           |
| **Content**        | The full text of the note                                                                            |
| **Tags**           | Colour-coded pills for filtering and organisation                                                    |
| **Captured by**    | A bookmark icon means you created it manually; a sparkle icon means Huginn captured it automatically |
| **Date**           | When the note was created                                                                            |
| **Source channel** | Whether the note came from the web dashboard or Telegram                                             |

## Cross-channel access

Your knowledge base is tied to your Huginn account, not to any specific channel. Notes created or captured during a Telegram conversation are immediately visible on the web dashboard, and notes you create on the web are available when Huginn responds in Telegram.

<Callout type="info">
  You can start a thought in Telegram and continue it on the web — Huginn has the full context
  regardless of where you're chatting.
</Callout>
