The Chat page is where you have conversations with Huginn directly in your browser. Responses stream in word by word, so you see the answer as it's being written — no waiting for a full response to appear at once.

## Starting a conversation

Navigate to **Chat** from the top nav. The page loads a fresh conversation thread. Type your message in the input area at the bottom and press **Enter** or click the send button.

## How conversations work

Each visit to the Chat page begins a new conversation thread. Huginn uses the full history of messages within that thread to keep the conversation coherent — you can refer back to something you said earlier and Huginn will understand.

<Callout type="info">
  Huginn's memory goes beyond the current conversation. It uses **semantic recall** to surface
  relevant context from past conversations, so it may remember things you discussed in previous
  sessions without you having to repeat them.
</Callout>

## What Huginn knows during chat

When you send a message, Huginn has access to:

- **Your personality files** — your SOUL and IDENTITY define how it responds and how it relates to you
- **Your calendar** — if you have a Google Calendar connected, Huginn knows today's events and can reference them naturally
- **Working memory** — persistent notes the AI keeps about your priorities, deadlines, and active threads
- **Past conversation context** — relevant snippets from previous chats, retrieved via semantic search

## Asking about your schedule

You don't need to paste your calendar into the chat. If you have a calendar connected, Huginn already knows today's events. For other dates, just ask — for example:

- "What do I have tomorrow afternoon?"
- "Am I free next Tuesday?"
- "What's on my calendar this week?"

Huginn uses a dedicated calendar tool to look up events for any date range you ask about.

## Chatting on mobile

The Chat page works in mobile browsers, but for the best mobile experience consider linking your [Telegram account](/docs/features/telegram) — the Telegram app gives you a native mobile interface with the same AI behind it.
