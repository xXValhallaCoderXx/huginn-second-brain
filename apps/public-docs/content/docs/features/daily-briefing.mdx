The Daily Briefing gives you a tailored overview of your day, generated by Huginn and delivered straight to your Telegram. It combines your calendar events with context from your conversation history to produce something that's actually useful — not just a list of meetings.

## Requesting a briefing

The daily briefing is on-demand. You ask for it when you want it.

<Steps>
<Step>
### Open the Huginn bot in Telegram

Make sure your [Telegram account is linked](/docs/features/telegram) first. Open the Huginn bot — the same one you chat with normally.

</Step>
<Step>
### Send `/brief`

Type `/brief` and send it. Huginn will show a typing indicator while it prepares your briefing.

</Step>
<Step>
### Read your briefing

In a few seconds, you'll receive a message with your personalised briefing. It typically includes:

- An overview of your calendar events for today
- Relevant context drawn from your recent conversations
- Any notes or priorities Huginn has in its working memory for you

</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  The briefing is generated fresh each time you request it — Huginn fetches your current calendar
  and recalls relevant memory at the moment you send `/brief`.
</Callout>

## What goes into a briefing

Huginn generates your briefing by running a multi-step process:

1. **Fetch today's calendar** — pulls all events for the current day from your connected calendars.
2. **Search conversation memory** — performs a semantic search over your past conversations, looking for context related to today's events and your current priorities.
3. **Generate the briefing** — your AI assistant writes the briefing using your personality files (SOUL + IDENTITY), so it's written in the voice and style you've configured.
4. **Deliver via Telegram** — the finished briefing is sent as a message to your Telegram chat.

## Prerequisites

You need two things for `/brief` to work:

- **A linked Telegram account** — see [Connect Telegram](/docs/features/telegram).
- **A connected Google Calendar** (recommended) — the briefing works without a calendar, but it's most useful when calendar events are included. See [Google Calendar Integration](/docs/features/calendar).

## Tips

**Use it as a morning ritual.** Send `/brief` when you start your day to get oriented before jumping into work.

**Update your personality files.** The briefing is shaped by your SOUL and IDENTITY files. If you've described your communication preferences, your role, or what a good briefing looks like, Huginn incorporates that into how it writes.

**Your calendar matters.** The more your calendar reflects your real schedule, the more grounded and useful the briefing will be.
