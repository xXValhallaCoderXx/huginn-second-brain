## What makes Huginn different

**One personality, everywhere.** You define your AI's character once. That same personality follows you across the web dashboard, Telegram, and any other channel you connect.

**A memory that grows.** Huginn remembers what you've talked about, learns your preferences over time, and surfaces relevant context when it matters.

**Your schedule, in context.** Connect your Google Calendar and Huginn automatically knows what you have on today — no need to paste your agenda into every conversation.

**Your morning briefing, on demand.** Ask Huginn for a `/brief` in Telegram and get a personalized summary of your day, generated from your calendar and conversation history.
