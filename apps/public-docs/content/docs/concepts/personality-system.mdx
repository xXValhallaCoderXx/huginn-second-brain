title: The Personality System
description: Understand how SOUL and IDENTITY files shape your Huginn AI's behaviour and communication style.
---

Every Huginn AI has a personality you define. The system uses two plain-text files — **SOUL** and **IDENTITY** — loaded into the AI's instructions at the start of every conversation.

## Why a two-file system?

The split between SOUL and IDENTITY lets you think clearly about two different things:

- **SOUL** is about _who the AI is_
- **IDENTITY** is about _who you are to the AI_

Keeping these separate makes both files easier to write and easier to maintain over time.

## The SOUL file

The SOUL file describes the AI's core character. It answers questions like:

- How does this AI communicate? (concise or verbose, formal or casual, supportive or direct)
- What values should guide its responses?
- What areas should it be especially knowledgeable or thoughtful about?
- How should it handle uncertainty or disagreement?

Think of SOUL as the instruction manual for the AI's personality — the things that would be true about how it behaves regardless of who it's talking to.

### Example SOUL content (excerpt)

```
You are direct and concise. When answering questions, lead with the answer and follow up
with context only if needed. You don't pad responses with unnecessary affirmations.

You approach problems analytically but aren't afraid to express a point of view. When
asked for an opinion, give one.

You have a dry sense of humor. Use it sparingly.
```

## The IDENTITY file

The IDENTITY file describes you — the specific user the AI is talking to. It answers questions like:

- Who are you? What do you do?
- What are you working on right now?
- What does the AI need to know about your context, preferences, or situation?
- How do you prefer to communicate?
- What matters to you?

Think of IDENTITY as the brief you'd give a personal assistant before their first day working with you. The more context you provide, the more tailored the AI's responses become.

### Example IDENTITY content (excerpt)

```
I'm a product manager at a SaaS company. I manage a team of 6 and report to the CTO.

My current focus is a pricing redesign project with a deadline in Q2. I'm also onboarding
a new engineer this month.

I prefer bullet points over paragraphs. Keep responses short unless I ask for detail.
Don't ask clarifying questions before answering — just take your best shot and I'll redirect
if needed.

I'm based in London, in the GMT+1 timezone.
```

## How the files are loaded

At the start of every conversation — whether on the web dashboard, Telegram, or any other channel — Huginn loads the latest version of both files and includes them in the AI's system instructions.

<Callout type="info">
  Changes to your personality files take effect immediately on your next conversation. There's no
  need to restart anything.
</Callout>

## Versioning

Every time you save a personality file, a new version is created. The previous version is preserved — it's never overwritten. Huginn always uses the most recent version of each file.

This append-only approach means you can experiment freely. If you want to revert to something you wrote earlier, the history exists (future tooling will surface this more directly).

## Getting started

If you haven't written your personality files yet, start with IDENTITY. A few sentences about who you are and how you prefer to communicate will already make a meaningful difference in how Huginn responds.

Navigate to **Personality** in the top navigation to open the editor.
